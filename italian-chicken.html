ITALIAN CHICKEN

ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1 cup italian dressing
  • 1 cup grated parmesan chesse
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • salt and pepper to taste

method of preparation

  1. marinate chicken breast in italian dressing for at least 2 hours
  2. Preheat your oven to 425degF (220degC)
  3. in a seperate bowl, combine parmesan chesse, garlic powder, basil oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. coat each chicken breast with the chesse mixture
  5. place the chicken on a baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and golden brown. enjoy!
