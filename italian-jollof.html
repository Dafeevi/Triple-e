ITALIAN JOLLOF

ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup of uncooked long-grain rice
  • 2 cup of water
  • 1 large onoin, chopped
  • 2 cloved garlic,minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 can (28oz) crused tomatoe
  • 1 teaspoon tomatoe paste
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

method of preparation

  1. heat olive oil in a large pan
  2. Add choppe onion, minced garlic and grated ginger
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoe and tomatoe paste.cook for a few minutes
  4. add uncooked long-grain rice,water,salt and black pepper
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat,cover,and simmer until rice is cooked and liquid is absorbed
