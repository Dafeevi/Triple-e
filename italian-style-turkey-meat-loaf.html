ITALIAN STYLE TURKEY

ingredients

  • 1 Ib ground turkey
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan chesse
  • 1/4 cup chooped fresh parsley
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 cup tomatoe sauce,divided
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella chesse

method of preparation

  1. pregeat your oven to 350degF (175degC)
  2. in a large bowl,combine the ground turkey, breadcrumbs,grated parmesan chesse, chopped parsley, beaten egg, salt,pepper, and half of the tomatoe sauce. mix until well combined.
  3. transfer the mixture to a loaf pan and shape into a loaf
  4. spread the remaining tomato sauce over the top of the loaf
  5. sprinkle the shredded mozzarella chesse evenly over the sauce
  6. bake for about 1 hour
  7. let it rest for few minutes before serving.
